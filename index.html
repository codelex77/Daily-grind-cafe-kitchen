About Us

More Than
Just Coffee

Located on Independence Avenue, we've become Gaborone's local institution. Whether you're here for an after-work drink, a quick business lunch, or a leisurely family breakfast — our space is designed to feel like home.

Every cup is crafted with care, every dish prepared fresh. We source our beans from East African farms and our produce locally, because quality starts at the source.
